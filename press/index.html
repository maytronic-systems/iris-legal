IRIS (Insight & Recon Interface System) is an Android app by Maytronic Systems that visualizes app permissions, network usage, and activity signals. IRIS focuses on neutral analytics and transparent dashboards so users can better understand what is happening on their devices.

IRIS is designed as an analysis and visualization tool and does not claim to block, remove, or interfere with other apps.
